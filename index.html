Dr Mohsin Raza(SMR)

Director at EDLAB Pakistan

Hi, Mohsin Raza,Director & Co-founder EDLAB Pakistan

Dr. Mohsin is an experienced trainer who has worked with teachers and professionals across diverse industries since last 10 years. He strongly believes in learning with high energy engagement and tools to build new skills. He is driven by his passion to help leaders and organizations bringing out their best. His training methodology involves storytelling, experiential learning and hands-on skill development work with learners. Dr. Mohsin has been appreciated for his programs on Emotional Intelligence, Stress Management, Team Building, Interpersonal Skills, Gamification, Communication Skills, Life Balance, Conflict resolutions, etc. He has trained and worked with 1800+ professionals and educationists. He is a certified Train the Trainer from Carnelian and has attended numerous programs on experiential learning.

Presently he is a Director at EDLAB Pakistan, an organization working on capacity building of youth and teachers. During his rich experience as a trainer, teacher, mentor and career counsellor, he has trained and conducted numerous programs which have turned out to be paradigm-shifting for his audiences.
